Current Company: Margulis Jewelerswww.margulis.com

Margulis Jewelers is a small, independent jeweler located at the heart of downtown Portland, OR. They have been in business since 1932 and boast creative excellence and professionalism. In mid August 2016, I released the first version of their website I was asked to recreate.

For this project, I had to start anew. I decided to use Wordpress as my CMS, since I am passing on the website administration services when leaving the company. I also chose new hosting services, and a theme (Xtheme). From working on this project I learned how to work in a small company, how to use wordpress, how to choose the right plugins, how to keep the company's vision in mind when designing, and so much more.. I've mostly gained skills in CSS and Wordpress.

These two frames are live versions of margulis.com before and after I was asked to recreate it. The new website is fully responsive, resize your browser window to see! For a better experience, visit www.margulis.com directly.
